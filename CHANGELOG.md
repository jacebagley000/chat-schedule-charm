# Changelog

## Week of May 25 – May 29, 2026

### New features

- **Live Schedule view, grouped by staff.** A new real-time schedule page shows your day at a glance with each staff member in their own column. Drag any appointment between columns or time slots to reassign or reschedule it — changes save instantly and broadcast to everyone watching. A **Live** indicator confirms you're connected to realtime updates. See [Your appointment calendar](https://fda-fdc26128.mintlify.app/appointments/calendar).
- **Use FrontDesk AI in your language.** The dashboard, calendar, inbox, and setup flows are now available in 12 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Japanese, Chinese, Arabic (with right-to-left support), Russian, and Polish. Switch languages anytime from the language picker in the header.

### Bug fixes

- **More reliable reconnection on the schedule view.** If your connection drops, the schedule now clearly shows an **Offline** state and automatically reconnects when your network is back — no refresh needed.

## Week of May 18 – May 22, 2026

### Updates

- **Clearer delete confirmation for appointments.** When you delete an appointment from the calendar, FrontDesk AI now shows an explicit confirmation prompt before removing it, with a reminder that deletion is permanent. If you want to keep the record, mark the appointment as **Cancelled** or **No show** instead. See [Deleting an appointment](https://fda-fdc26128.mintlify.app/appointments/booking#deleting-an-appointment).
