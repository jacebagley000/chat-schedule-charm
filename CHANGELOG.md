# Changelog

## Week of May 18 – May 22, 2026

### Updates

- **Clearer delete confirmation for appointments.** When you delete an appointment from the calendar, FrontDesk AI now shows an explicit confirmation prompt before removing it, with a reminder that deletion is permanent. If you want to keep the record, mark the appointment as **Cancelled** or **No show** instead. See [Deleting an appointment](https://fda-fdc26128.mintlify.app/appointments/booking#deleting-an-appointment).
